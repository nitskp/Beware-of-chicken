Characters

Jin Rou

Jin is 6'2 tall, with short, brown hair. His jaw is more square, and more obviously masculine than most cultivators go for. His skin is lightly tanned, and he has a few freckles on his cheeks.

The most noticeable things about him are his green eyes, and the big dumb grin on his face. Jin smiles a lot, and not fake smiles, but the ones of a man genuinely enjoying his life. His eyes are bright, and he looks happy.

Big D

The Magnificent cock, the First Disciple of Fa Ram. A rooster the likes of which are unmatched. His plumage looks like captures fire. his proud tail shimmers like gemstones, his beak and spurs are sharp and Iron-like.

He wears a dapper fox-fur vest, and a small silver necklace, a gift from Zhang Fei the Torrent Rider.

Hong Meiling

Originally from Hong Yaowu, daughter of the Village Chief Hong Xian. A woman 5'2 in height, with green tinted hair and narrow violet eyes. A bridge of freckles spreads across her nose and cheeks. She is generally a slim woman, with a bit of working muscle on her, and lightly tanned skin.

She wears a green shirt, and a long pink skirt. Her hair is done up into a bun in the back with two chopsticks, aand she has two other strands of hair that rest on her shoulders, tied with pink bows.

Rou Tigu

A girl who was once a cat, ascended to human form out of her desire to communicate. Tigu Is a bright looking young woman with orange hair and yellow eyes. Her skin is deeply tanned. She is quite heavily muscled for a woman, with visible abs, and strong arms.

She normally wears only one of Jin's stolen shirts, which is obviously too big for her, bandages around her chest, and sandals.

Author

Casualfarmer :

I've always loved stories. It comes from my father, and his own imagination. Every night before bed, he used to tell me a new chapter of Harry The Rock Hound, and his adventures through space to find new and interesting asteroids. Dear old Harry may mostly be lost to my memories, for he was never written down, but the sense of wonder remained. I want to tell my own stories. Writing is my hobby, and I love it.

My current story is "Beware of Chicken", an Original Xianxia story that pokes a bit of fun at a lot of the tropes. Its going to hopefully continue for a while, and I hope you can enjoy the journey with me. Beware of Chicken is posted on Questionable Questing, Spacebattles, and Royal Road. All posts will be posted here as well.
